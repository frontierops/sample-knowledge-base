# Gungan

Gungans are an amphibious humanoid species native to [Naboo](../planets/naboo.md), where they live in
domed cities beneath the planet's lakes and swamps. Tall and long-eared, they are equally at home in
water and on land.

For generations the Gungans kept apart from Naboo's human population, and the two societies regarded each
other with mutual suspicion. That changed when a Trade Federation invasion threatened both peoples:
[Padmé Amidala](../characters/padme-amidala.md) forged an alliance, and the Gungan Grand Army took the
field against the Federation's droids while the humans retook their capital. The Gungans fielded
energy-shield technology and rode tamed beasts into battle, and their cooperation marked a turning point
in Naboo's modern history within the [Galactic Republic](../factions/galactic-republic.md).
