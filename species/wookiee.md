# Wookiee

Wookiees are a species of tall, powerfully built, furred bipeds native to the forest world of
[Kashyyyk](../planets/kashyyyk.md). Standing well over two meters, they possess immense physical strength
and a fierce loyalty to those they trust, bound by a tradition known as the life debt.

Wookiees speak Shyriiwook, a language of roars and growls that most cannot reproduce but many can learn
to understand. Despite their intimidating appearance, they are intelligent and capable engineers and
pilots. The most famous Wookiee, [Chewbacca](../characters/chewbacca.md), served as co-pilot to
[Han Solo](../characters/han-solo.md). Under the [Galactic Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md),
Wookiees were enslaved in great numbers for their strength.
