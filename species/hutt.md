# Hutt

Hutts are a species of large, slug-like beings native to the world of Nal Hutta. Long-lived and slow,
they have broad bodies, thick tails, and wide mouths, and they lack legs, moving by slithering or by
repulsorlift sleds.

Hutts are infamous across the galaxy for their dominance of organized crime. Cunning, patient, and
ruthless, they built the [Hutt Cartel](../factions/hutt-cartel.md) into a criminal empire spanning the
Outer Rim. The most notorious Hutt, Jabba, ruled a crime operation from
[Tatooine](../planets/tatooine.md), employing bounty hunters like
[Boba Fett](../characters/boba-fett.md) and holding [Han Solo](../characters/han-solo.md) captive.
Hutts speak Huttese, a language widely used in the galaxy's criminal underworld.
