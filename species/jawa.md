# Jawa

Jawas are a species of small, hooded scavengers native to the desert world of
[Tatooine](../planets/tatooine.md). Rarely seen beneath their rough brown robes, they are known for
their glowing yellow eyes and their chittering, rapid-fire language, Jawaese.

Jawas survive by scavenging the desert for discarded technology, scrap, and especially
[droids](../technology/astromech-droid.md), which they repair and sell from their massive, treaded
sandcrawlers. It was Jawas who captured [R2-D2](../characters/r2-d2.md) and
[C-3PO](../characters/c-3po.md) and sold them to the Lars moisture farm, setting
[Luke Skywalker](../characters/luke-skywalker.md) on his path. Shrewd and opportunistic traders, Jawas
are famed for selling equipment that works just long enough to complete the sale.
