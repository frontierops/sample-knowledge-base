# Twi'lek

Twi'leks are a humanoid species native to the harsh world of Ryloth, instantly recognizable by the pair
of fleshy "head-tails," called lekku, that extend from the backs of their skulls. Their skin comes in a
wide range of colors, including blue, green, red, and yellow.

Lekku are used both for balance and for a subtle gestural language. Twi'leks spread across the galaxy as
traders, entertainers, and laborers, and were often exploited — many were enslaved by criminal groups
such as the [Hutt Cartel](../factions/hutt-cartel.md) or oppressed under the
[Galactic Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md). A number of Twi'leks were Force-sensitive and served
in the [Jedi Order](../factions/jedi-order.md).
