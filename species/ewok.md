# Ewok

Ewoks are a species of small, furred, bipedal natives of the forest moon of
[Endor](../planets/endor.md), living in tribal villages built among the branches of the moon's giant
trees. Though technologically primitive, relying on spears, bows, and traps, they are brave, resourceful,
and fiercely protective of their home.

Ewoks played an unexpectedly decisive role in the [Galactic Civil War](../events/galactic-civil-war.md).
When a [Rebel Alliance](../factions/rebel-alliance.md) strike team arrived on Endor, the Ewoks — having
come to revere the droid [C-3PO](../characters/c-3po.md) as a deity — allied with the Rebels. Their
ambush of Imperial forces helped the team destroy the shield generator protecting the second
[Death Star](../technology/death-star.md) at the [Battle of Endor](../events/battle-of-endor.md).
