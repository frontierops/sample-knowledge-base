# Coruscant

Coruscant is a Core World whose entire surface has been covered by a single, planet-wide city — an
ecumenopolis of towering skyscrapers, layered traffic lanes, and undercity depths that never see the
sun. Home to trillions, it sits at the symbolic and political center of the galaxy.

For a thousand generations Coruscant served as the capital of the
[Galactic Republic](../factions/galactic-republic.md) and the seat of its Senate. The
[Jedi Order](../factions/jedi-order.md) maintained its great Temple here. When
[Palpatine](../characters/palpatine.md) transformed the Republic into the
[Galactic Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md), Coruscant became the Imperial capital, renamed
Imperial Center in official usage. Whoever controlled Coruscant was widely seen to control the galaxy.
