# Hoth

Hoth is a frozen world of glaciers, ice plains, and perpetual blizzards in the Outer Rim. Its
inhospitable surface is home to few creatures — among them the predatory wampa and the herd-dwelling
tauntaun, which the [Rebel Alliance](../factions/rebel-alliance.md) used as mounts.

After the [Battle of Yavin](../events/battle-of-yavin.md), the Rebels established their hidden Echo Base
on Hoth. The Empire eventually located it, leading to the [Battle of Hoth](../events/battle-of-hoth.md),
in which Imperial walkers overran the base and forced the Rebels to evacuate.
[Luke Skywalker](../characters/luke-skywalker.md) survived a wampa attack and a night exposed to the
cold before departing to seek out [Yoda](../characters/yoda.md).
