# Naboo

Naboo is a green, peaceful world of rolling hills, lakes, and classical architecture. It is home to two
societies: a human population governed by an elected monarch, and the amphibious
[Gungans](../species/gungan.md), who dwell in domed cities beneath the planet's waters.

Naboo's modern history is shaped by two of its natives. [Padmé Amidala](../characters/padme-amidala.md)
served as its Queen and later Senator, while [Sheev Palpatine](../characters/palpatine.md) used a Trade
Federation blockade and invasion of Naboo as the crisis that launched his rise to Supreme Chancellor.
The human and Gungan peoples, long estranged, united to repel that invasion in a battle that marked the
[Galactic Republic](../factions/galactic-republic.md)'s slide toward war.
