# Tatooine

Tatooine is a harsh desert planet in the Outer Rim Territories, orbiting a pair of suns, Tatoo I and
Tatoo II. Covered in sand dunes, rocky canyons, and salt flats, it is sparsely populated and largely
beyond the reach of central galactic authority.

## Society

With little water and no significant central government, Tatooine's economy runs on moisture farming,
scavenging, and crime. The [Hutt Cartel](../factions/hutt-cartel.md), led on-world by Jabba the Hutt,
holds effective power over much of the planet's underworld. Spaceports like Mos Eisley are notorious
havens for smugglers and bounty hunters such as [Boba Fett](../characters/boba-fett.md). The native
[Jawas](../species/jawa.md) scavenge droids and scrap, while the nomadic Tusken Raiders, or Sand
People, roam the deep desert.

## Significance

Tatooine looms large in galactic history despite its remoteness. [Anakin Skywalker](../characters/darth-vader.md)
was born here a slave, and decades later his son [Luke Skywalker](../characters/luke-skywalker.md) was
raised on a moisture farm by the Lars family. [Obi-Wan Kenobi](../characters/obi-wan-kenobi.md) lived
here in exile, watching over the boy. It was on Tatooine that Luke acquired the droids
[R2-D2](../characters/r2-d2.md) and [C-3PO](../characters/c-3po.md) and first met Obi-Wan, beginning
his journey into [the Force](../concepts/the-force.md).
