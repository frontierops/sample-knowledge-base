# Bespin

Bespin is a gas giant whose habitable band of atmosphere hosts Cloud City, a floating colony that mines
valuable tibanna gas and serves as a resort and trade hub. The colony operated with relative
independence from the [Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md), administered by
[Lando Calrissian](../characters/lando-calrissian.md).

Bespin became a trap in the Galactic Civil War when [Darth Vader](../characters/darth-vader.md) occupied
Cloud City to lure [Luke Skywalker](../characters/luke-skywalker.md). There Vader froze
[Han Solo](../characters/han-solo.md) in carbonite and revealed to Luke that he was his father.
[Leia Organa](../characters/leia-organa.md), [Chewbacca](../characters/chewbacca.md), and Lando escaped
aboard the [*Millennium Falcon*](../technology/millennium-falcon.md) as the Empire tightened its grip on
the city.
