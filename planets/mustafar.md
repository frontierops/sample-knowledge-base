# Mustafar

Mustafar is a volcanic world of lava rivers, ash fields, and constant eruptions in the Outer Rim. Its
hellish surface and rich mineral deposits made it useful for mining, but it is best remembered for one
catastrophic duel.

After [Order 66](../events/order-66.md), the newly fallen [Anakin Skywalker](../characters/darth-vader.md)
came to Mustafar. There his former master [Obi-Wan Kenobi](../characters/obi-wan-kenobi.md) confronted
him. Obi-Wan defeated Anakin on the lava banks, leaving him grievously burned — injuries that required
the life-support armor by which Anakin would forever after be known as Darth Vader. Mustafar later
became the site of one of Vader's personal fortresses, a place strong in the dark side of
[the Force](../concepts/the-force.md).
