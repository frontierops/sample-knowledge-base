# Dagobah

Dagobah is a remote, swamp-covered world of bogs, fog, and dense jungle, untouched by industry or
settlement. It is strong in [the Force](../concepts/the-force.md), with a notable dark-side presence in
a cave near where [Yoda](../characters/yoda.md) made his home.

After the fall of the [Jedi Order](../factions/jedi-order.md), Yoda chose Dagobah for his exile
precisely because its isolation and Force-rich nature helped conceal him from the
[Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md). It was here that [Luke Skywalker](../characters/luke-skywalker.md)
came to complete his Jedi training, guided by the spirit of
[Obi-Wan Kenobi](../characters/obi-wan-kenobi.md).
