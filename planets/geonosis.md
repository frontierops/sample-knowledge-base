# Geonosis

Geonosis is a rocky, arid world of harsh deserts and towering rock spires, home to the insectoid
Geonosians who live in hive colonies. Their vast droid foundries made the planet an industrial center
for the [Confederacy of Independent Systems](../factions/confederacy-of-independent-systems.md).

Geonosis is best known as the place where the [Clone Wars](../events/clone-wars.md) began. The
[Battle of Geonosis](../events/battle-of-geonosis.md) saw the [Galactic Republic](../factions/galactic-republic.md)
deploy its new clone army for the first time, rescuing a group of Jedi captured by the Separatists. It
was also on Geonosis that early designs for the [Death Star](../technology/death-star.md) superweapon
were conceived.
