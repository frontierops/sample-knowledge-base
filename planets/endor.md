# Endor

Endor — more precisely the forest moon of Endor — is a lush, wooded world of towering trees and
sprawling forests in the Outer Rim. It is the home of the [Ewoks](../species/ewok.md), a species of
small, furred, technologically primitive but resourceful natives.

The [Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md) built a shield generator on the moon's surface to protect
the second [Death Star](../technology/death-star.md) in orbit above. At the
[Battle of Endor](../events/battle-of-endor.md), a [Rebel](../factions/rebel-alliance.md) strike team
led by [Han Solo](../characters/han-solo.md) — allied with the Ewoks — destroyed the generator, allowing
the Rebel fleet to attack and destroy the station.
