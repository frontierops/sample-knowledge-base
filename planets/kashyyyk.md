# Kashyyyk

Kashyyyk is a lush forest world covered by colossal wroshyr trees that rise hundreds of meters, their
canopies forming the layered cities of the [Wookiees](../species/wookiee.md). The Wookiees, including
[Chewbacca](../characters/chewbacca.md), are the planet's native species.

A battle was fought on Kashyyyk during the closing days of the [Clone Wars](../events/clone-wars.md),
with [Yoda](../characters/yoda.md) himself aiding the Wookiee defense. Under the
[Galactic Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md), Kashyyyk suffered greatly: the Empire enslaved much
of its population, exploiting Wookiee strength for forced labor across the galaxy.
