# Alderaan

Alderaan was a serene, mountainous Core World known for its culture, art, and commitment to pacifism.
It had no standing military and was admired throughout the galaxy as a beacon of civilization. Its royal
house produced [Leia Organa](../characters/leia-organa.md), adopted daughter of Senator Bail Organa.

Despite its peaceful reputation, Alderaan was a quiet center of support for the
[Rebel Alliance](../factions/rebel-alliance.md). To demonstrate the power of the first
[Death Star](../technology/death-star.md) — and to break Leia — [Grand Moff Tarkin](../characters/grand-moff-tarkin.md)
ordered the planet's [destruction](../events/destruction-of-alderaan.md). Alderaan was annihilated with
its billions of inhabitants, an atrocity that galvanized galactic resistance to the Empire.
