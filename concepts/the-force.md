# The Force

The Force is an energy field generated by all living things that binds the galaxy together. It is the
source of the supernatural abilities wielded by the [Jedi](../factions/jedi-order.md) and the
[Sith](../factions/sith.md), and understanding it is central to both orders.

## Light and dark

The Force is commonly described as having two aspects. The light side is associated with peace,
knowledge, serenity, and selflessness — the path of the Jedi, codified in the
[Jedi Code](jedi-code.md). The dark side is fueled by fear, anger, hatred, and passion — the path of the
Sith. The dark side offers quicker, more seductive power, but it corrupts those who use it.
[Yoda](../characters/yoda.md) warned that fear, anger, and hate were the steps that led to the dark side.

## Powers and sensitivity

Those strong in the Force can sense thoughts and emotions, move objects with their minds, glimpse
possible futures, and enhance their physical abilities. Sensitivity to the Force tends to run in
bloodlines — most famously the Skywalker family, including
[Anakin](../characters/darth-vader.md), [Luke](../characters/luke-skywalker.md), and
[Leia](../characters/leia-organa.md). A prophecy spoke of a "Chosen One" who would bring balance to the
Force, a role ultimately fulfilled by Anakin Skywalker when he destroyed the Sith Emperor.
