# Kyber Crystal

A kyber crystal is a rare, Force-attuned crystal that lies at the heart of every
[lightsaber](../technology/lightsaber.md), focusing its energy into a coherent blade. Kyber is deeply
connected to [the Force](the-force.md), and the crystals are sometimes described as living things.

A [Jedi](../factions/jedi-order.md) traditionally journeyed to find their own crystal, which was
colorless until bonded to its wielder, whereupon it took on a color — most often blue or green. The
[Sith](../factions/sith.md), lacking crystals of their own, would dominate a kyber crystal through the
dark side until it "bled," turning the blade red. Beyond personal weapons, kyber's immense capacity to
store and amplify energy made it a target of the [Galactic Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md), which
harvested enormous quantities of it to power the planet-destroying superlaser of the
[Death Star](../technology/death-star.md).
