# T-65 X-wing Starfighter

The T-65 X-wing was a single-seat starfighter and the workhorse of the
[Rebel Alliance](../factions/rebel-alliance.md)'s starfighter corps. It took its name from the
S-foils — split wings that lock open into an X-shape during combat to spread its four laser cannons and
maximize their field of fire.

The X-wing balanced firepower, speed, and durability, and carried proton torpedoes for attacking
capital ships and hardened targets. It seated an astromech [droid](astromech-droid.md), such as
[R2-D2](../characters/r2-d2.md), behind the cockpit to manage repairs and navigation.
[Luke Skywalker](../characters/luke-skywalker.md) flew an X-wing at the
[Battle of Yavin](../events/battle-of-yavin.md), using its torpedoes to destroy the first
[Death Star](death-star.md). It was a frequent opponent of the Empire's
[TIE fighters](tie-fighter.md).
