# TIE Fighter

The TIE fighter — short for Twin Ion Engine — was the standard starfighter of the
[Galactic Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md), instantly recognizable by its ball-shaped cockpit
flanked by two hexagonal solar collector panels. Its distinctive screaming engine note made it one of
the most feared sounds in Imperial space.

TIE fighters were fast, agile, and cheap to mass-produce, but they sacrificed shields and a hyperdrive
to achieve it, relying instead on overwhelming numbers and the support of nearby
[Star Destroyers](star-destroyer.md). They were the natural counterpart to the Rebel
[X-wing](x-wing.md). Specialized variants existed, including the TIE Advanced flown by
[Darth Vader](../characters/darth-vader.md) at the [Battle of Yavin](../events/battle-of-yavin.md).
