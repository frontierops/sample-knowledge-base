# Astromech Droid

An astromech droid is a versatile utility droid designed to maintain and repair starships,
calculate hyperspace jumps, and store large amounts of data. Compact and rugged, astromechs are
equipped with a wide array of retractable tools concealed within their cylindrical bodies.

In starfighters such as the [X-wing](x-wing.md), an astromech rides in an external socket, making
in-flight repairs and managing navigation so the pilot can focus on combat. The most famous astromech,
[R2-D2](../characters/r2-d2.md), repeatedly proved how resourceful these droids could be, often paired
with the protocol droid [C-3PO](../characters/c-3po.md). Though built as tools, many astromechs
developed distinct personalities over years of service, especially when their memories were never wiped.
