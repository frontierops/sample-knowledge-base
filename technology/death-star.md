# Death Star

The Death Star was a moon-sized battle station and the [Galactic Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md)'s
ultimate symbol of power, armed with a superlaser capable of destroying an entire planet in a single
shot. Two were built during the Galactic Civil War.

## The first Death Star

Conceived from designs traced back to [Geonosis](../planets/geonosis.md), the first Death Star was
commanded by [Grand Moff Tarkin](../characters/grand-moff-tarkin.md). To demonstrate its power, the
Empire used it to carry out the [destruction of Alderaan](../events/destruction-of-alderaan.md). The
station had one fatal flaw: a small thermal exhaust port leading directly to its reactor. At the
[Battle of Yavin](../events/battle-of-yavin.md), [Luke Skywalker](../characters/luke-skywalker.md) fired
the proton torpedoes that triggered the chain reaction and destroyed it.

## The second Death Star

The Empire began building a larger, more powerful second Death Star over the forest moon of
[Endor](../planets/endor.md), protected by a deflector shield projected from the surface. At the
[Battle of Endor](../events/battle-of-endor.md), Rebel forces destroyed the shield generator and then
flew into the station's superstructure to destroy its reactor core, obliterating the unfinished station
along with much of the Imperial command.
