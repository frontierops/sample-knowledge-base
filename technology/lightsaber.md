# Lightsaber

A lightsaber is an elegant energy weapon consisting of a plasma blade projected from a metal hilt. It is
the signature weapon of both the [Jedi](../factions/jedi-order.md) and the [Sith](../factions/sith.md),
described as "an elegant weapon for a more civilized age."

## Construction and the crystal

At the heart of every lightsaber is a [kyber crystal](../concepts/kyber-crystal.md), a Force-attuned
crystal that focuses the blade's energy and determines its color. Jedi traditionally bore blue or green
blades, while the Sith produced red blades by "bleeding" their crystals through the dark side of
[the Force](../concepts/the-force.md). Rare colors existed too — [Mace Windu](../characters/mace-windu.md)
famously wielded a purple blade. A Jedi constructed their own lightsaber as a rite of passage.

## Use

The blade can cut through nearly any material and deflect [blaster](blaster.md) bolts, making a skilled
wielder formidable against many opponents at once. Mastery required years of training in the
[combat forms](../concepts/lightsaber-combat-forms.md) and a deep connection to the Force, since a
duelist relied on the Force to anticipate an opponent's moves.
