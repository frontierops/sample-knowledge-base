# Star Destroyer

The Star Destroyer was the iconic wedge-shaped capital ship of the
[Galactic Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md), forming the backbone of the Imperial Navy. The
common Imperial-class measured over a kilometer and a half in length and bristled with turbolaser
batteries.

A single Star Destroyer carried thousands of troops, a full wing of [TIE fighters](tie-fighter.md),
walkers, and landing craft, making it capable of subjugating a planet on its own. Its silhouette,
descending from above, was a deliberate instrument of intimidation. [Darth Vader](../characters/darth-vader.md)
commanded the fleet from the *Executor*, a Super Star Destroyer many times larger than the standard
vessel. The Empire's mass production of these warships gave it a numerical advantage the
[Rebel Alliance](../factions/rebel-alliance.md) could rarely match in open battle.
