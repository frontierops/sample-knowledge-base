# Hyperdrive

The hyperdrive is the propulsion technology that allows starships to cross the vast distances between
star systems by entering hyperspace, an alternate dimension in which faster-than-light travel is
possible. Without it, interstellar civilization on a galactic scale would be impossible.

A hyperdrive must follow precalculated routes, because stars, planets, and other masses cast "shadows"
into hyperspace that a ship cannot pass through — hence the need for accurate navigational data before a
jump. Hyperdrives are rated by class, with a lower number indicating a faster drive; the
[*Millennium Falcon*](millennium-falcon.md) was famed for its exceptionally fast, if temperamental,
modified hyperdrive. Smaller fighters like the [X-wing](x-wing.md) carried their own hyperdrives, while
the Empire's [TIE fighters](tie-fighter.md) generally did not, tethering them to capital ships such as
[Star Destroyers](star-destroyer.md).
