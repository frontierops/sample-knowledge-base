# Blaster

A blaster is a ranged energy weapon that fires concentrated bolts of plasma, and it is by far the most
common weapon in the galaxy. Blasters range from compact hold-out pistols to heavy repeating weapons,
with the blaster pistol and blaster rifle being the most widespread.

Imperial stormtroopers carried standard-issue blaster rifles, while figures like
[Han Solo](../characters/han-solo.md) favored a personal blaster pistol. A blaster bolt can be deflected
by a [lightsaber](lightsaber.md) in skilled hands, one reason a lone Jedi could stand against a squad of
blaster-armed soldiers. Specialized bounty hunters such as
[Boba Fett](../characters/boba-fett.md) carried heavily customized blaster weaponry.
