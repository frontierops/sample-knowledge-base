# Millennium Falcon

The *Millennium Falcon* was a heavily modified Corellian YT-1300 light freighter, outwardly a battered
cargo hauler but secretly one of the fastest ships in the galaxy. Its pilot
[Han Solo](../characters/han-solo.md) boasted that it made the Kessel Run in less than twelve parsecs.

Originally owned by [Lando Calrissian](../characters/lando-calrissian.md), the *Falcon* passed to Han,
who flew it with his co-pilot [Chewbacca](../characters/chewbacca.md). Beneath its worn hull sat a
souped-up [hyperdrive](hyperdrive.md), upgraded weapons, and a maze of smuggling compartments. The ship
carried [Luke Skywalker](../characters/luke-skywalker.md) and [Obi-Wan Kenobi](../characters/obi-wan-kenobi.md)
off [Tatooine](../planets/tatooine.md), escaped the Empire at [Bespin](../planets/bespin.md), and — flown
by Lando — delivered the killing blow to the second [Death Star](death-star.md) at the
[Battle of Endor](../events/battle-of-endor.md).
