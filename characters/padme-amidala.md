# Padmé Amidala

Padmé Amidala was a human stateswoman from [Naboo](../planets/naboo.md), serving first as the
planet's elected Queen and later as its Senator in the [Galactic Republic](../factions/galactic-republic.md).
A principled defender of democracy, she opposed the accumulation of emergency powers during the
[Clone Wars](../events/clone-wars.md).

Padmé secretly married the Jedi [Anakin Skywalker](darth-vader.md), a union forbidden by the
[Jedi Order](../factions/jedi-order.md). She was the mother of the twins [Luke Skywalker](luke-skywalker.md)
and [Leia Organa](leia-organa.md). As the Republic became the [Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md)
under [Palpatine](palpatine.md), Padmé died shortly after the twins were born — and her children were
hidden and separated to keep them safe from the new Sith order.
