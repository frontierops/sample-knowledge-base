# Grand Moff Tarkin

Wilhuff Tarkin was a human Imperial officer who held the rank of Grand Moff, governing the Outer Rim
Territories for the [Galactic Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md). He was the architect of the
"Tarkin Doctrine," the idea that the Empire could rule through fear of overwhelming force rather than
constant deployment of it.

Tarkin commanded the first [Death Star](../technology/death-star.md), and it was he who ordered the
[destruction of Alderaan](../events/destruction-of-alderaan.md) to demonstrate the station's power and
intimidate the [Rebel Alliance](../factions/rebel-alliance.md). He dismissed the Rebel assault on the
station as futile and remained aboard, dying when [Luke Skywalker](luke-skywalker.md) destroyed the
Death Star at the [Battle of Yavin](../events/battle-of-yavin.md). He frequently commanded the service
of [Darth Vader](darth-vader.md).
