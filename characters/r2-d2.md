# R2-D2

R2-D2 was an astromech [droid](../technology/astromech-droid.md) known for resourcefulness and
courage well beyond his utilitarian design. Paired with the protocol droid [C-3PO](c-3po.md), "Artoo"
served across decades of galactic conflict.

He carried the stolen [Death Star](../technology/death-star.md) plans entrusted to him by
[Leia Organa](leia-organa.md), setting in motion the events of the
[Battle of Yavin](../events/battle-of-yavin.md). As [Luke Skywalker](luke-skywalker.md)'s astromech,
R2-D2 rode in the socket of Luke's [X-wing](../technology/x-wing.md), repairing the fighter in flight
and aiding the Rebellion at nearly every turn of the war.
