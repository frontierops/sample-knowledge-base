# Obi-Wan Kenobi

Obi-Wan Kenobi was a human Jedi Master and one of the most renowned members of the
[Jedi Order](../factions/jedi-order.md). He trained [Anakin Skywalker](darth-vader.md) and, decades
later, set [Luke Skywalker](luke-skywalker.md) on the path to becoming a Jedi.

## The Clone Wars

As a Jedi General, Obi-Wan led [Republic](../factions/galactic-republic.md) clone forces across the
[Clone Wars](../events/clone-wars.md), often fighting alongside his former Padawan Anakin. He was known
for his mastery of [lightsaber](../technology/lightsaber.md) defense and his diplomatic patience.

## Exile and return

When Anakin fell to the dark side, Obi-Wan defeated him in a duel on
[Mustafar](../planets/mustafar.md), leaving him for dead. Obi-Wan then went into hiding on
[Tatooine](../planets/tatooine.md), watching over the infant Luke. Years later he answered a distress
message from [Leia Organa](leia-organa.md), reentered the conflict, and gave Luke his first lessons in
[the Force](../concepts/the-force.md). Aboard the [Death Star](../technology/death-star.md), Obi-Wan
allowed [Darth Vader](darth-vader.md) to strike him down, becoming one with the Force and continuing to
guide Luke as a spectral presence.
