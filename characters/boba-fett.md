# Boba Fett

Boba Fett was a human bounty hunter, regarded as one of the most effective in the galaxy. He was an
unaltered clone of the bounty hunter Jango Fett, whose genetic template was also used to create the
[Republic](../factions/galactic-republic.md)'s clone army for the [Clone Wars](../events/clone-wars.md).
Jango raised Boba as a son.

Wearing distinctive [Mandalorian](../factions/mandalorians.md)-style armor with a jetpack and an array
of concealed weapons, Boba worked for clients ranging from the [Galactic Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md)
to the crime lord Jabba of the [Hutt Cartel](../factions/hutt-cartel.md). [Darth Vader](darth-vader.md)
employed him to track the [*Millennium Falcon*](../technology/millennium-falcon.md), leading to the
capture of [Han Solo](han-solo.md), whom Boba delivered to Jabba frozen in carbonite.
