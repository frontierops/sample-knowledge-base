# Lando Calrissian

Lando Calrissian was a human gambler, smuggler, and entrepreneur who became the Baron Administrator of
Cloud City on [Bespin](../planets/bespin.md). An old friend — and rival — of [Han Solo](han-solo.md),
Lando once owned the [*Millennium Falcon*](../technology/millennium-falcon.md) before losing it to Han
in a game of sabacc.

When the [Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md) occupied Cloud City to set a trap for Han, Lando was
coerced into cooperating, but he turned against the Empire to help [Leia Organa](leia-organa.md) and
[Chewbacca](chewbacca.md) escape. He then joined the [Rebel Alliance](../factions/rebel-alliance.md),
and as a general he piloted the *Millennium Falcon* into the second
[Death Star](../technology/death-star.md) to destroy its reactor core at the
[Battle of Endor](../events/battle-of-endor.md).
