# Darth Vader

Darth Vader was the Sith identity of **Anakin Skywalker**, a former Jedi Knight who became the most
feared enforcer of the [Galactic Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md). Once prophesied to bring
balance to [the Force](../concepts/the-force.md), Anakin fell to the dark side and served the
[Sith](../factions/sith.md) Lord [Palpatine](palpatine.md) as his apprentice, Darth Vader.

## From Jedi to Sith

Anakin began life as a slave on [Tatooine](../planets/tatooine.md) before being discovered and trained
by [Obi-Wan Kenobi](obi-wan-kenobi.md). His secret marriage to [Padmé Amidala](padme-amidala.md) and
his fear of losing her made him vulnerable to Palpatine's manipulation. During [Order 66](../events/order-66.md)
he turned against the [Jedi Order](../factions/jedi-order.md). Obi-Wan defeated him in a duel on
[Mustafar](../planets/mustafar.md), where Anakin's wounds and burns left him dependent on the black
armor and respirator that defined Vader for the rest of his life.

## Service to the Empire

As Vader, he hunted the surviving Jedi and pursued the [Rebel Alliance](../factions/rebel-alliance.md).
He commanded from the Super Star Destroyer *Executor* and answered directly to the Emperor. Vader did
not know, at first, that the Rebel pilot who destroyed the [Death Star](../technology/death-star.md) at
the [Battle of Yavin](../events/battle-of-yavin.md) was his own son, [Luke Skywalker](luke-skywalker.md).

## Redemption

At [Bespin](../planets/bespin.md), Vader revealed his paternity to Luke. At the
[Battle of Endor](../events/battle-of-endor.md), watching Palpatine torture his son, the man within the
armor reasserted himself. Anakin killed the Emperor, fulfilling the prophecy and dying as he turned
back to the light.
