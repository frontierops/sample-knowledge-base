# Leia Organa

Leia Organa was a human female leader of the [Rebel Alliance](../factions/rebel-alliance.md) and a
princess of [Alderaan](../planets/alderaan.md). Born to [Padmé Amidala](padme-amidala.md) and
[Anakin Skywalker](darth-vader.md), she was adopted by Senator Bail Organa and raised in the Alderaanian
royal house. She was the twin sister of [Luke Skywalker](luke-skywalker.md).

## Rebellion

As a member of the Imperial Senate, Leia secretly worked for the Rebellion, smuggling the stolen
[Death Star](../technology/death-star.md) plans inside the astromech [R2-D2](r2-d2.md). Captured by
[Darth Vader](darth-vader.md), she was forced to watch as the Empire destroyed Alderaan, her home
world. Rescued by Luke, [Han Solo](han-solo.md), and [Chewbacca](chewbacca.md), she became a central
figure in the Alliance's command.

Leia led from the front at the [Battle of Hoth](../events/battle-of-hoth.md) and helped infiltrate
the Empire's shield bunker on [Endor](../planets/endor.md). She was Force-sensitive, like her brother
and father, though she did not train as a [Jedi](../factions/jedi-order.md) during the Galactic Civil
War. She and Han Solo became one of the era's defining partnerships.
