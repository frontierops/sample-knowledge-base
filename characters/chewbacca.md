# Chewbacca

Chewbacca was a [Wookiee](../species/wookiee.md) from the forest world of
[Kashyyyk](../planets/kashyyyk.md) and the longtime companion and co-pilot of
[Han Solo](han-solo.md). Standing well over two meters tall and immensely strong, "Chewie" was a skilled
mechanic, marksman, and gunner aboard the [*Millennium Falcon*](../technology/millennium-falcon.md).

Bound to Han by a Wookiee life debt, Chewbacca fought for the
[Rebel Alliance](../factions/rebel-alliance.md) throughout the Galactic Civil War, from the rescue of
[Leia Organa](leia-organa.md) to the [Battle of Endor](../events/battle-of-endor.md). He communicated
in the roaring Wookiee language Shyriiwook, which his crewmates understood even though he could not
speak Galactic Basic.
