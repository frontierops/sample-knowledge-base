# Mace Windu

Mace Windu was a human Jedi Master and one of the most senior members of the Jedi High Council,
second in stature only to [Yoda](yoda.md) within the [Jedi Order](../factions/jedi-order.md). He was
renowned as one of the greatest [lightsaber](../technology/lightsaber.md) duelists of his age, wielding
a distinctive amethyst-bladed weapon and mastering the aggressive combat form Vaapad.

A general during the [Clone Wars](../events/clone-wars.md), Windu grew increasingly suspicious of
Supreme Chancellor [Palpatine](palpatine.md). When [Anakin Skywalker](darth-vader.md) revealed that
Palpatine was a Sith Lord, Windu led a team to arrest him. He defeated Palpatine in single combat — but
at the decisive moment Anakin intervened, allowing the Chancellor to kill Windu and accelerating the
Jedi Order's fall.
