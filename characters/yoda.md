# Yoda

Yoda was the Grand Master of the [Jedi Order](../factions/jedi-order.md), a being of a small, unnamed
species who lived for roughly 900 years and trained Jedi for some eight centuries. He was one of the
most powerful practitioners of [the Force](../concepts/the-force.md) in galactic history.

During the [Clone Wars](../events/clone-wars.md), Yoda served as a general of the
[Republic](../factions/galactic-republic.md). When [Order 66](../events/order-66.md) destroyed the
Order, he confronted [Emperor Palpatine](palpatine.md) and, unable to defeat him, went into exile on
the swamp world of [Dagobah](../planets/dagobah.md).

There he lived in hiding until [Luke Skywalker](luke-skywalker.md) sought him out for training, on the
guidance of [Obi-Wan Kenobi](obi-wan-kenobi.md)'s spirit. Yoda instructed Luke in patience, discipline,
and the nature of the Force before dying of old age, becoming one with the Force as Obi-Wan had.
