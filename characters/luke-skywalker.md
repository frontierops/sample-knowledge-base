# Luke Skywalker

Luke Skywalker was a human male who rose from a moisture farm on [Tatooine](../planets/tatooine.md)
to become one of the most pivotal [Jedi](../factions/jedi-order.md) in galactic history. He was the
son of [Anakin Skywalker](darth-vader.md) and [Padmé Amidala](padme-amidala.md), and the twin brother
of [Leia Organa](leia-organa.md) — a parentage hidden from him through his youth.

## Early life

Raised by his uncle Owen and aunt Beru on the Lars homestead, Luke dreamed of leaving Tatooine to
join the [Rebel Alliance](../factions/rebel-alliance.md). His path turned when he acquired two droids,
[C-3PO](c-3po.md) and [R2-D2](r2-d2.md), the latter carrying stolen plans for the
[Death Star](../technology/death-star.md). This drew him to [Obi-Wan Kenobi](obi-wan-kenobi.md), who
gave Luke his father's [lightsaber](../technology/lightsaber.md) and began his instruction in
[the Force](../concepts/the-force.md).

## The Galactic Civil War

Flying an [X-wing](../technology/x-wing.md) for the Rebellion, Luke fired the proton torpedoes that
destroyed the first Death Star at the [Battle of Yavin](../events/battle-of-yavin.md). He later
sought out the exiled Jedi Master [Yoda](yoda.md) on [Dagobah](../planets/dagobah.md) to complete his
training. At [Bespin](../planets/bespin.md), [Darth Vader](darth-vader.md) revealed himself to be
Luke's father.

At the [Battle of Endor](../events/battle-of-endor.md), Luke surrendered to the Empire to confront
[Emperor Palpatine](palpatine.md). Refusing to give in to the dark side, he appealed to the good still
within his father — and Vader turned on the Emperor, destroying him at the cost of his own life.
With the Emperor dead, Luke became the first of a new generation of Jedi.
