# Han Solo

Han Solo was a human smuggler and pilot who became a hero of the
[Rebel Alliance](../factions/rebel-alliance.md). With his Wookiee co-pilot
[Chewbacca](chewbacca.md), he flew the [*Millennium Falcon*](../technology/millennium-falcon.md), a
freighter he won from [Lando Calrissian](lando-calrissian.md).

Initially a cynical mercenary in debt to the crime lord Jabba the Hutt of the
[Hutt Cartel](../factions/hutt-cartel.md), Han was hired by [Luke Skywalker](luke-skywalker.md) and
[Obi-Wan Kenobi](obi-wan-kenobi.md) to reach [Alderaan](../planets/alderaan.md). He returned
unexpectedly at the [Battle of Yavin](../events/battle-of-yavin.md) to clear Luke's path to destroy
the [Death Star](../technology/death-star.md).

Over the war Han grew into a committed Rebel and a general, leading the strike team that disabled the
shield generator at the [Battle of Endor](../events/battle-of-endor.md). His romance with
[Leia Organa](leia-organa.md) endured despite his being frozen in carbonite and held by Jabba between
the battles of [Hoth](../events/battle-of-hoth.md) and Endor.
