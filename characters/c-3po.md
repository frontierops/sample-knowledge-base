# C-3PO

C-3PO was a humanoid protocol [droid](../technology/astromech-droid.md) designed for etiquette,
translation, and protocol, fluent in over six million forms of communication. Anxious and fussy by
nature, he was the near-constant companion of the astromech [R2-D2](r2-d2.md).

Built by a young [Anakin Skywalker](darth-vader.md) on [Tatooine](../planets/tatooine.md), C-3PO
eventually came into the service of the [Rebel Alliance](../factions/rebel-alliance.md), accompanying
[Luke Skywalker](luke-skywalker.md) and [Leia Organa](leia-organa.md) through the Galactic Civil War.
On [Endor](../planets/endor.md), the [Ewoks](../species/ewok.md) mistook him for a deity, an
accident that helped win their alliance with the Rebels.
