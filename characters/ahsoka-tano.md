# Ahsoka Tano

Ahsoka Tano was a Togruta female and the Padawan apprentice of [Anakin Skywalker](darth-vader.md)
during the [Clone Wars](../events/clone-wars.md). Trained from a young age in the
[Jedi Order](../factions/jedi-order.md), she fought alongside Anakin and
[Obi-Wan Kenobi](obi-wan-kenobi.md) and earned a reputation for independence and skill with dual
[lightsabers](../technology/lightsaber.md).

Wrongly accused of a bombing and expelled before being cleared, Ahsoka chose to walk away from the
Order, disillusioned by its rigidity. Because she had left, she was not present for
[Order 66](../events/order-66.md) and survived the purge of the Jedi. She continued to fight tyranny in
the years that followed, an enduring example of a Force-user outside the formal structures of the Jedi
and the [Sith](../factions/sith.md).
