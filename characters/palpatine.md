# Sheev Palpatine

Sheev Palpatine was a human male from [Naboo](../planets/naboo.md) who concealed his identity as the
[Sith](../factions/sith.md) Lord **Darth Sidious**. Through patient manipulation he orchestrated the
collapse of the [Galactic Republic](../factions/galactic-republic.md) and reconstituted it as the
[Galactic Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md), which he ruled as Emperor.

## The grand plan

Palpatine's rise is among the most consequential arcs in galactic history. As Darth Sidious he followed
the [Rule of Two](../concepts/the-sith-rule-of-two.md), the Sith principle of one master and one
apprentice. His apprentice Darth Maul provoked the Trade Federation's blockade of Naboo, while his
public persona — Senator Palpatine — leveraged the resulting crisis to be elected Supreme Chancellor.

He then engineered the [Clone Wars](../events/clone-wars.md), secretly directing both the Republic and
the [Confederacy of Independent Systems](../factions/confederacy-of-independent-systems.md). The war
justified emergency powers that he never relinquished. Sidious also corrupted
[Anakin Skywalker](darth-vader.md), exploiting the Jedi's fear of loss to turn him into the Sith
apprentice [Darth Vader](darth-vader.md).

## Emperor

When [Mace Windu](mace-windu.md) attempted to arrest him, Palpatine revealed his power and, with
Anakin's help, killed the Jedi Master. He issued [Order 66](../events/order-66.md), the command that
turned the clone troopers against their Jedi generals, and declared the first Galactic Empire with
himself as Emperor. [Yoda](yoda.md) failed to defeat him in single combat and fled into exile.

## Downfall

For decades Palpatine ruled through fear, the [Imperial Senate](../factions/galactic-empire.md), and
the threat of the [Death Star](../technology/death-star.md). He sought to turn
[Luke Skywalker](luke-skywalker.md) to the dark side at the
[Battle of Endor](../events/battle-of-endor.md). When Luke refused, Palpatine tried to kill him — and
Darth Vader, the redeemed Anakin Skywalker, threw the Emperor down a reactor shaft, ending his reign.
