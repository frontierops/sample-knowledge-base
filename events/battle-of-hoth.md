# Battle of Hoth

The Battle of Hoth was a major [Imperial](../factions/galactic-empire.md) victory in the
[Galactic Civil War](galactic-civil-war.md), in which Imperial forces discovered and assaulted the
[Rebel Alliance](../factions/rebel-alliance.md)'s hidden Echo Base on the ice world of
[Hoth](../planets/hoth.md).

Tracking the Rebels with a probe droid, [Darth Vader](../characters/darth-vader.md) led a fleet to Hoth.
A line of towering AT-AT walkers advanced on the base's shield generator while Rebel snowspeeders fought
a desperate delaying action. The Empire broke through, but the bulk of the Alliance — including
[Leia Organa](../characters/leia-organa.md) and [Han Solo](../characters/han-solo.md) — escaped. The
battle scattered the Rebel leadership and sent [Luke Skywalker](../characters/luke-skywalker.md) to
[Dagobah](../planets/dagobah.md) to seek out [Yoda](../characters/yoda.md), while Han fled toward
[Bespin](../planets/bespin.md).
