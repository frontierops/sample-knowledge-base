# The Clone Wars

The Clone Wars were a galaxy-spanning conflict between the
[Galactic Republic](../factions/galactic-republic.md) and the
[Confederacy of Independent Systems](../factions/confederacy-of-independent-systems.md). The war takes
its name from the clone army secretly created to fight for the Republic.

## Course of the war

The war began with the [Battle of Geonosis](battle-of-geonosis.md) and raged for roughly three years
across countless worlds. The Republic's clone troopers, led by [Jedi](../factions/jedi-order.md)
generals such as [Obi-Wan Kenobi](../characters/obi-wan-kenobi.md) and
[Anakin Skywalker](../characters/darth-vader.md), fought the Separatists' droid armies. The Jedi, long a
peacekeeping order, were transformed into wartime commanders.

## A war by design

Unknown to nearly everyone, both sides were manipulated by the same hand:
[Palpatine](../characters/palpatine.md), secretly the Sith Lord Darth Sidious. He engineered the
conflict to accrue emergency powers, exhaust and discredit the Jedi, and position himself to seize total
control. The war ended with [Order 66](order-66.md) and the proclamation of the
[Galactic Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md) — exactly the outcome Sidious had planned.
