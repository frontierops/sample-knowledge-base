# Order 66

Order 66 was the secret command that directed the [Galactic Republic](../factions/galactic-republic.md)'s
clone troopers to turn on and execute their [Jedi](../factions/jedi-order.md) commanders. Its execution
brought about the near-total destruction of the Jedi Order.

The order was issued at the climax of the [Clone Wars](clone-wars.md) by Supreme Chancellor
[Palpatine](../characters/palpatine.md) — secretly the Sith Lord Darth Sidious — once
[Anakin Skywalker](../characters/darth-vader.md) had fallen to the dark side. Across the galaxy, clones
who had fought beside the Jedi suddenly gunned them down. Thousands of Jedi died within hours. A handful
survived, including [Yoda](../characters/yoda.md), [Obi-Wan Kenobi](../characters/obi-wan-kenobi.md),
and [Ahsoka Tano](../characters/ahsoka-tano.md), most going into deep hiding. With the Jedi eliminated
as a check on his power, Palpatine declared the [Galactic Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md).
