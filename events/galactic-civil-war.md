# Galactic Civil War

The Galactic Civil War was the conflict between the [Rebel Alliance](../factions/rebel-alliance.md) and
the [Galactic Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md), fought to end the tyranny of
[Emperor Palpatine](../characters/palpatine.md) and restore democratic government to the galaxy.

The war's defining moments form a clear arc. The Alliance struck its first decisive blow at the
[Battle of Yavin](battle-of-yavin.md), destroying the first [Death Star](../technology/death-star.md).
The Empire struck back at the [Battle of Hoth](battle-of-hoth.md), overrunning the Rebels' Echo Base.
The war reached its climax at the [Battle of Endor](battle-of-endor.md), where the Alliance destroyed
the second Death Star and Palpatine was killed. Endor broke the Empire's grip on the galaxy and opened
the way for a New Republic, though scattered Imperial remnants fought on for years afterward.
