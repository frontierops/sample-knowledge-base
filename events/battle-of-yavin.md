# Battle of Yavin

The Battle of Yavin was the first major victory of the [Rebel Alliance](../factions/rebel-alliance.md)
in the [Galactic Civil War](galactic-civil-war.md), in which Rebel starfighters destroyed the first
[Death Star](../technology/death-star.md). The battle is so pivotal that the wider galaxy later dated
events relative to it.

## The assault

After [R2-D2](../characters/r2-d2.md) delivered the stolen Death Star plans, Rebel analysts identified a
single weakness: a small thermal exhaust port that led directly to the station's reactor. A strike force
of [X-wing](../technology/x-wing.md) and Y-wing fighters attacked as the Death Star moved to destroy the
Rebel base on Yavin's fourth moon.

## The decisive shot

With [Grand Moff Tarkin](../characters/grand-moff-tarkin.md) refusing to evacuate, the battle came down
to a final trench run. [Darth Vader](../characters/darth-vader.md) pursued the attackers and nearly
stopped them, until [Han Solo](../characters/han-solo.md) returned in the
[*Millennium Falcon*](../technology/millennium-falcon.md) to drive him off. Trusting the
[Force](../concepts/the-force.md) over his targeting computer,
[Luke Skywalker](../characters/luke-skywalker.md) fired the proton torpedoes that triggered the reactor
chain reaction, destroying the Death Star moments before it could fire on the moon.
