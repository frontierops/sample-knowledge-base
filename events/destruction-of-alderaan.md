# Destruction of Alderaan

The destruction of [Alderaan](../planets/alderaan.md) was one of the great atrocities of the Galactic
Civil War, in which the [Galactic Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md) used the first
[Death Star](../technology/death-star.md) to obliterate an entire inhabited planet.

[Grand Moff Tarkin](../characters/grand-moff-tarkin.md) ordered the strike to demonstrate the station's
power and to pressure his captive, [Leia Organa](../characters/leia-organa.md), into revealing the
location of the [Rebel Alliance](../factions/rebel-alliance.md)'s hidden base. Although Leia named a
decoy location, Tarkin destroyed Alderaan anyway. Billions died in an instant on a world famous for its
pacifism and beauty. Rather than cow the galaxy into submission, the atrocity hardened resistance to the
Empire and lent moral urgency to the Rebel victory soon after at the
[Battle of Yavin](battle-of-yavin.md).
