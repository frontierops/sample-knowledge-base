# Battle of Geonosis

The Battle of Geonosis was the first battle of the [Clone Wars](clone-wars.md), fought on the desert
world of [Geonosis](../planets/geonosis.md) between the [Galactic Republic](../factions/galactic-republic.md)
and the [Confederacy of Independent Systems](../factions/confederacy-of-independent-systems.md).

The battle began when a large force of [Jedi](../factions/jedi-order.md) attempted to rescue captured
comrades from the Separatists' droid foundries and arena. Heavily outnumbered, the Jedi were on the
verge of defeat when [Yoda](../characters/yoda.md) arrived with the newly created clone army — the first
time these soldiers took the field. The clones and their Jedi generals drove the Separatists back, but
the engagement marked the start of a galaxy-wide war and the transformation of the Jedi from
peacekeepers into military commanders.
