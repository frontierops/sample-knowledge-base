# Battle of Endor

The Battle of Endor was the decisive engagement of the [Galactic Civil War](galactic-civil-war.md), in
which the [Rebel Alliance](../factions/rebel-alliance.md) destroyed the second
[Death Star](../technology/death-star.md), killed [Emperor Palpatine](../characters/palpatine.md), and
broke the back of the [Galactic Empire](../factions/galactic-empire.md).

## Three battles at once

The engagement unfolded on three fronts. On the forest moon of [Endor](../planets/endor.md), a strike
team led by [Han Solo](../characters/han-solo.md) — aided by the [Ewoks](../species/ewok.md) — worked to
destroy the bunker projecting the Death Star's protective shield. In orbit, the Rebel fleet found the
Death Star's shield still active and was forced into a close-quarters fight with the Imperial Navy,
springing the Emperor's trap. Aboard the station, [Luke Skywalker](../characters/luke-skywalker.md)
confronted [Darth Vader](../characters/darth-vader.md) and the Emperor.

## Outcome

When the shield finally fell, [Lando Calrissian](../characters/lando-calrissian.md) flew the
[*Millennium Falcon*](../technology/millennium-falcon.md) into the station to destroy its reactor core.
Refusing to turn to the dark side, Luke was tortured by Palpatine — until Vader, the redeemed
[Anakin Skywalker](../characters/darth-vader.md), threw the Emperor to his death. The Death Star
exploded, and the galaxy celebrated the fall of the Empire.
