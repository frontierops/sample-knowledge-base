# Mandalorians

The Mandalorians were a warrior culture originating on the planet Mandalore, defined not by species but
by a shared creed, language, and martial tradition. Members of many species could become Mandalorian by
adopting its way of life.

Mandalorians were famed for their distinctive armor, often forged from the near-indestructible metal
beskar, and for their combat prowess with [blasters](../technology/blaster.md), jetpacks, and an array
of integrated weapons. Their history included long and bitter wars with the [Jedi](jedi-order.md). The
bounty hunter [Boba Fett](../characters/boba-fett.md) wore Mandalorian-style armor. Under the
[Galactic Empire](galactic-empire.md), Mandalore was conquered and its people scattered and persecuted,
though their culture endured.
