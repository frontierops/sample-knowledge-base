# Galactic Republic

The Galactic Republic was the democratic union of star systems that governed much of the galaxy for a
thousand generations before its fall. Its Senate met on [Coruscant](../planets/coruscant.md), and it
relied on the [Jedi Order](jedi-order.md) as guardians of peace rather than a standing army.

In its final decades the Republic grew corrupt and gridlocked. A separatist movement, the
[Confederacy of Independent Systems](confederacy-of-independent-systems.md), broke away, plunging the
Republic into the [Clone Wars](../events/clone-wars.md). Supreme Chancellor
[Palpatine](../characters/palpatine.md) — secretly the Sith Lord directing both sides — used the war to
accumulate emergency powers. At its end he reorganized the Republic into the first
[Galactic Empire](galactic-empire.md), ending a thousand years of democracy. Decades later, after the
Empire's defeat, a New Republic would rise to take its place.
