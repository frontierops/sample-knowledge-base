# Confederacy of Independent Systems

The Confederacy of Independent Systems, also called the Separatist Alliance, was a coalition of star
systems, corporations, and trade bodies that seceded from the
[Galactic Republic](galactic-republic.md). Publicly led by the former Jedi Count Dooku, it drew support
from worlds disillusioned with Republic corruption.

The Confederacy fielded vast armies of battle droids, manufactured in foundries on worlds such as
[Geonosis](../planets/geonosis.md). Its secession triggered the [Clone Wars](../events/clone-wars.md),
which opened with the [Battle of Geonosis](../events/battle-of-geonosis.md). What its member worlds did
not know was that the Confederacy was secretly controlled by Darth Sidious —
[Palpatine](../characters/palpatine.md) — the very same Sith Lord leading the Republic against them. By
manipulating both sides, he ensured a war that would deliver him absolute power.
