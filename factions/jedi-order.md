# Jedi Order

The Jedi Order was an ancient monastic order of Force-sensitives devoted to the light side of
[the Force](../concepts/the-force.md), serving as guardians of peace and justice in the
[Galactic Republic](galactic-republic.md) for a thousand generations.

## Way of the Jedi

Jedi trained from childhood, progressing from youngling to Padawan to Knight and, for some, to Master.
They lived by the [Jedi Code](../concepts/jedi-code.md), which counseled against fear, anger, and
attachment, and wielded [lightsabers](../technology/lightsaber.md) built around
[kyber crystals](../concepts/kyber-crystal.md). The Order was led by a High Council that included
[Yoda](../characters/yoda.md) and [Mace Windu](../characters/mace-windu.md).

## Destruction

The Order was nearly annihilated during the [Clone Wars](../events/clone-wars.md) when
[Palpatine](../characters/palpatine.md) issued [Order 66](../events/order-66.md), turning the clone
troopers against their Jedi generals. Survivors like [Obi-Wan Kenobi](../characters/obi-wan-kenobi.md)
and Yoda went into hiding. The Order's hopes passed to a new generation through
[Luke Skywalker](../characters/luke-skywalker.md). The Jedi's ancient rivals were the
[Sith](sith.md), dark-side users who sought the Order's destruction.
