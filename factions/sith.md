# The Sith

The Sith were an ancient order of Force-users devoted to the dark side of
[the Force](../concepts/the-force.md), drawing power from passion, anger, and ambition. They were the
mortal enemies of the [Jedi Order](jedi-order.md), and their conflict spanned millennia.

After catastrophic wars in which the Sith nearly destroyed themselves through infighting, the Sith Lord
Darth Bane established the [Rule of Two](../concepts/the-sith-rule-of-two.md): only ever two Sith at a
time, a master to embody power and an apprentice to crave it. Operating in secret for a thousand years,
the Sith engineered their revenge under Darth Sidious — [Palpatine](../characters/palpatine.md) — and his
apprentice [Darth Vader](../characters/darth-vader.md). They wielded red-bladed
[lightsabers](../technology/lightsaber.md), the color a product of "bled" synthetic crystals. The Sith
were finally defeated when Vader killed the Emperor at the
[Battle of Endor](../events/battle-of-endor.md).
