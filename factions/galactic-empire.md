# Galactic Empire

The Galactic Empire was the autocratic regime that replaced the
[Galactic Republic](galactic-republic.md), founded and ruled by [Emperor Palpatine](../characters/palpatine.md).
It governed through fear, centralized control, and overwhelming military force.

## Founding

The Empire was proclaimed by Palpatine in the wake of the [Clone Wars](../events/clone-wars.md), after
he issued [Order 66](../events/order-66.md) to destroy the [Jedi Order](jedi-order.md). The Senate,
weary of war, greeted the transformation with applause. Palpatine — secretly the [Sith](sith.md) Lord
Darth Sidious — recast the Republic's institutions as instruments of his personal rule, with
[Darth Vader](../characters/darth-vader.md) as his enforcer.

## Rule and military

The Empire projected power through a vast military: legions of stormtroopers, fleets of
[Star Destroyers](../technology/star-destroyer.md), and ultimately the [Death Star](../technology/death-star.md),
a battle station capable of destroying entire planets. The "Tarkin Doctrine," championed by
[Grand Moff Tarkin](../characters/grand-moff-tarkin.md), held that fear of such force would keep the
galaxy in line. The destruction of [Alderaan](../planets/alderaan.md) was meant to prove it.

## Decline

Imperial tyranny provoked the [Rebel Alliance](rebel-alliance.md). After the Rebels destroyed the first
Death Star at the [Battle of Yavin](../events/battle-of-yavin.md) and the second, along with the Emperor
and Vader, at the [Battle of Endor](../events/battle-of-endor.md), the Empire fractured and gave way to
the [New Republic](galactic-republic.md).
