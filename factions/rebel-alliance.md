# Rebel Alliance

The Alliance to Restore the Republic, commonly called the Rebel Alliance, was a coalition of star
systems, defectors, and freedom fighters united in armed resistance to the
[Galactic Empire](galactic-empire.md). Its goal was to end Palpatine's tyranny and restore democratic
government.

Leaders such as [Leia Organa](../characters/leia-organa.md) and Mon Mothma coordinated a network of
cells operating from hidden bases, including Echo Base on [Hoth](../planets/hoth.md). The Alliance won
its first major victory at the [Battle of Yavin](../events/battle-of-yavin.md), where
[Luke Skywalker](../characters/luke-skywalker.md) destroyed the first [Death Star](../technology/death-star.md).
Despite a costly defeat at the [Battle of Hoth](../events/battle-of-hoth.md), the Alliance fleet
ultimately destroyed the second Death Star and killed the Emperor at the
[Battle of Endor](../events/battle-of-endor.md), paving the way for a New Republic. Its pilots flew
[X-wings](../technology/x-wing.md) and other starfighters against the Empire's superior numbers.
