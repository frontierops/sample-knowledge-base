# Hutt Cartel

The Hutt Cartel was a sprawling criminal syndicate controlled by the [Hutts](../species/hutt.md), a
species of large, slug-like beings notorious for greed and cunning. The Cartel dominated the underworld
of the Outer Rim, profiting from smuggling, slavery, gambling, and extortion.

Its most infamous figure was Jabba the Hutt, a crime lord who ruled from a palace on
[Tatooine](../planets/tatooine.md). Jabba employed bounty hunters such as
[Boba Fett](../characters/boba-fett.md) and held [Han Solo](../characters/han-solo.md) — frozen in
carbonite — as a prize after Han failed to repay a debt. The Cartel operated largely beyond the reach of
both the [Republic](galactic-republic.md) and the [Empire](galactic-empire.md), a power unto itself in
the lawless reaches of the galaxy.
